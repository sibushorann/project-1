About us


"Welcome to Twinkle and Tinsel, where the magic of Christmas comes to life! Our store is dedicated to helping you create a holiday season filled with warmth, joy, and sparkle. Whether you're looking for timeless ornaments, shimmering lights, or festive treasures to transform your home, we have something special for every style and celebration.


At Twinkle and Tinsel, we take pride in curating high-quality, unique decorations that bring a touch of wonder to your holiday traditions. Our mission is to make your Christmas unforgettable by offering products that inspire creativity and spread cheer. Step into our store and discover a world of twinkling lights, glimmering tinsel, and everything you need to make this season truly magical.


Whether you're decorating for a cozy family gathering or a grand holiday party, Twinkle and Tinsel is here to help you craft the perfect festive atmosphere. Let us be part of your holiday traditions, bringing warmth, wonder, and a touch of magic to your Christmas season.

Our Mission

Twinkle & Tinsel is your one-stop destination for all things festive and magical. From shimmering holiday decor to handcrafted ornaments, our shop brings the sparkle of the season to life. Whether you're hosting a cozy gathering or planning a grand celebration, we have everything to make your moments unforgettable.

Founded with a passion for creativity and a love for festivities, Twinkle & Tinsel curates unique and sustainable decor items that add charm to your celebrations. Our collection is sourced from talented artisans around the world, ensuring each piece tells a story of craftsmanship and tradition.

Did you know? Twinkle & Tinsel offers exclusive customization options, allowing you to create personalized decorations and gifts for your loved ones. Join our vibrant community of holiday enthusiasts and let us help you celebrate life's special moments in style!
